VIDEOS FOR HIMANI'S BIRTHDAY WEBSITE
==========================================

Place your actual videos in this folder. The website will automatically display:
- moment3.mp4 (video for third slot)
- moment5.mp4 (video for fifth slot)

Recommended video specifications:
- Format: MP4 (H.264 codec)
- Resolution: 1920x1080p or 1280x720p
- Duration: 10-60 seconds
- File size: Under 50MB for web loading
- Audio: Include if desired, or use muted videos

Simply drag and drop your videos here and rename them to match the filenames above.
