PHOTOS FOR HIMANI'S BIRTHDAY WEBSITE
==========================================

Place your actual photos in this folder. The website will automatically display:
- memory1.jpg (wide photo for first slot)
- moment2.jpg (standard photo)
- memory4.jpg (wide photo)
- moment6.jpg (standard photo)

Recommended photo sizes:
- Wide photos: 16:9 aspect ratio, minimum 1200x675px
- Standard photos: 4:3 aspect ratio, minimum 800x600px

Simply drag and drop your photos here and rename them to match the filenames above.
